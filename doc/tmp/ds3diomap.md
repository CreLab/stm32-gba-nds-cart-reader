All cycle timings are counted in 33.51MHz units. NORMAL commands takes
9..12 cycles, depending on the number of enabled lights in PolyAttr
(Huh, 9..12 (four timings) cycles for 0..4 (five settings) lights?)
Total execution time of SwapBuffers is Duration until VBlank, plus 392
cycles.
In MTX_MODE=2 (Simultanous Set), MTX_MULT/TRANS take additional 30
cycles.
