**Note**
- Operand \[FExx\] can he in range FE20h..FF1Fh, of which FE20h..FEFFh i
encoded as 20..FF, and FF00h..FF1Fh is encoded as 00..1F (the latter
- overlaps with operand \[FFxx\]) (for the overlapping addresses, \[FExx\
- is preferred because BT/SET1/CLR1 have smaller encoding for \[FExx\
- than \[FFxx\], and DJNZ/ALU support only \[FExx\]) (all opcodes tha
- support \[FFxx\] do also support \[FExx\], so \[FFxx\] should be NEVE
- used for \[FF00..FF1F\]).
